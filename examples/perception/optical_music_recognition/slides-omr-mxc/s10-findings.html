Key Findings

Architecture > Size

Qwen3.5-9B (9.5B) beats Qwen3-VL-32B (32B) — architecture revision matters more than 3.5× parameter count

Format is Critical

MXC enables note generation. XML never works — tokens consumed by verbose markup

Models < 8B Fail

3-4B models cannot learn pitch regardless of architecture or LoRA rank

Data is the Bottleneck

Model converged at 35% on 3K samples. More/better training data is the next lever
