MXC: MusicXML Compact

Design

  • Line-based encoding: one note = one line
  • N C4 q 10080 su L1:s:Lord, = quarter C4, stem up, lyric
  • 12x compression: 14K tokens → 1.2K tokens
  • 99.2% lossless round-trip on 500 samples

What It Preserves

  • Pitches, durations, stems, beams
  • Lyrics (bilingual, syllabic encoding)
  • Key/time signatures, clefs
  • Dynamics, directions, barlines
  • Composer, lyricist, title
