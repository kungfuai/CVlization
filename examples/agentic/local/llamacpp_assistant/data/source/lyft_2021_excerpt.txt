Lyft 2021 Financial Highlights:
- Revenue: $3.208 billion, up 35.7% year-over-year.
- Active riders: 18.7 million.
- Adjusted EBITDA loss narrowed to $76 million.
Key Drivers:
- Recovery of ride-hailing demand as pandemic restrictions eased.
- Growth in micro-mobility and driver incentives to rebuild supply.
- Focus on cost discipline and streamlined operations.
Risks and Outlook:
- Competitive pressure from Uber and regional players.
- Regulatory changes on worker classification and insurance costs.
- Continued investment required for autonomous technology partnerships.
