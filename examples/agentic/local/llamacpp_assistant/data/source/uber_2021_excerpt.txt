Uber 2021 Financial Highlights:
- Revenue: $17.455 billion, representing 56.7% year-over-year growth.
- Gross Bookings: $90.4 billion across Mobility and Delivery segments.
- Adjusted EBITDA turned positive at $1.0 billion in Q4.
Key Drivers:
- Strong recovery in Mobility as riders returned to cities and travel.
- Continued expansion of Uber Eats with new merchant partnerships.
- Freight segment growth through logistics acquisitions.
Risks and Outlook:
- Regulatory scrutiny on gig worker classification and regional compliance.
- Supply and demand balance as driver incentives normalize.
- Strategic focus on profitability and platform efficiency.
